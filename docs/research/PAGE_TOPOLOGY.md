# LovingMyCurves.com - Page Topology

## Site Overview
E-commerce lingerie site built on WordPress/WooCommerce. Primary brand color: #E6007E (hot pink/magenta).

## Page Sections (Top to Bottom)

### 1. Top Bar (Sticky)
- Contact info: "Sarit Centre: +254 746 844 227 Yaya Centre: +254 703 844 227"
- Social icons: Facebook, Instagram, Pinterest
- Right links: Our Story, Contact, Videos
- Background: Light gray
- **Interaction: Static**

### 2. Main Header (Sticky on scroll)
- Logo: MyCurves logo (500x145px)
- Main nav: HOME, BRAS, PANTIES, SHAPEWEAR, ON SALE
- CTA button: "Find Your Size" (pink button)
- Search bar
- Cart icon
- **Interaction: Sticky behavior on scroll**

### 3. Hero Slider (Full-width)
- Revolution Slider with multiple slides
- Background images of models wearing lingerie
- Overlay text: "Embrace your curves" / "Fall in love with yourself"
- **Interaction: Auto-rotating carousel**

### 4. Mission Statement Section
- White background
- Centered text block
- Heading: "We are a community..."
- Subheading: "Did you know? 80% of women wear wrong bra size"
- **Interaction: Static**

### 5. Category Cards (4-column grid)
- Know Your Size (call-to-action)
- Bras (product category with image)
- Sportswear (product category with image)
- Shapewear (product category with image)
- **Interaction: Hover effects on cards**

### 6. Featured Products Section
- Heading: "EMBRACE YOUR CURVES"
- 3 product cards in a row
- Product images, titles, prices in KSh
- **Interaction: Product hover effects**

### 7. Blog/Editorial Section (3-column)
- 3 article cards with images
- Titles: "Confidence In A Cup", "My biggest problem became my goldmine", "Booming Corsets Business"
- "Read More" links
- **Interaction: Card hover effects**

### 8. Partner Brands Logos (Carousel/Grid)
- Brand partner logos: Panache, Naomi & Nicole, Curvy Kate, Miraclesuit, Leonisa, Gorsenia
- **Interaction: May be carousel**

### 9. Instagram Feed Section
- Heading: "Our Social Media"
- Grid of Instagram posts (12 visible)
- "Follow on Instagram" button
- **Interaction: Instagram API integration**

### 10. Footer (Multi-column)
- Column 1: Useful Links (Find My Size, About, Our Story, Testimonials)
- Column 2: Customer Service (Help & FAQs, Contacts, Returns, Deliveries)
- Column 3: Opening Hours (Sarit Centre, Yaya Centre times)
- Column 4: Contact Info (Phone, Email for both locations)
- Social icons
- **Interaction: Static**

## Key Design Elements
- Primary color: #E6007E (brand pink)
- Fonts: Montserrat (headings), Open Sans (body)
- Rounded buttons with hover states
- Box shadows on cards
- WhatsApp floating button (bottom right)

## Mobile Considerations
- Header collapses to hamburger menu
- Hero slider maintains aspect ratio
- Category cards stack to 2 or 1 column
- Product grid adjusts to 2 or 1 column
- Footer stacks vertically

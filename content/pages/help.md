Do you offer free bra fittings?
Yes! We offer FREE professional bra fittings at both our Sarit Centre and Yaya Centre stores.

What sizes do you carry?
We specialize in extended sizing, including band sizes 32–48 and cup sizes A through H and beyond, depending on brand.

How do I know if I'm wearing the wrong size?
Signs include straps digging in, band riding up, cup spillage, or gaping cups. Visit our Find Your Size page or book an in-store fitting.

Can I exchange a bra?
Yes, subject to our returns policy. Hygiene seals must be intact and items unworn with tags attached.

Do you deliver?
Yes. See our Deliveries page for details.
